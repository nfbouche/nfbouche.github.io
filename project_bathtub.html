Galaxy self-regulation

The Lyman-α line is the strongest tracer of neutral hydrogen in the Universe, but its origin is still under debate.

The Bathtub/Regulator Model

The bathtub model is a useful tool to study the scaling relations of star-forming galaxies such as the galaxy main-sequence and the Tully-Fischer relation.

Details about the methodology can be found Bouché et al. 2010
