The Origin of the Lyman-α Emission

The Lyman-α line is the strongest tracer of neutral hydrogen in the Universe, but its origin is still under debate.

The Bathtub Model

The bathtub model is used to study the Lyman-α polarization and discriminate between different scenarios for its origin.
